Who is Gearmo?

Gearmo is a long time user of the internet and has explored all the corners and features that the web has to offer. From being a professional rhythm gamer to being a social media micro celebrity, Gearmo aspires to give back to the people of the internet and aide with their business needs. He seeks to put himself in his clients shoes and see what steps must be taken to find success in the work they do.

One outlook that has helped Gearmo push himself is the concept of a growth mindset. He believes that you can only get better the more you do something. Gearmo strives on repetition and self-discipline to help establish good business practices, and hopes never settle with what he already knows -- rather, to continue learning forever.

The Steel Bridge is a through truss, double-deck vertical-lift bridge across the Willamette River in

Portland, OR, opened in 1912. Its lower deck carries railroad and bicycle/pedestrian traffic, while the

upper deck carries road traffic and light rail, making the bridge one of the most multimodal in the

world. It is the only double-deck bridge with independent lifts in the world and the second oldest

vertical-lift bridge in North America, after the nearby Hawthorne Bridge.

The Japanese Friendship Garden, also known as Sankei-en ( 三渓園 ) is a twelve-acre Japanese

garden located within Balboa Park in San Diego, California. It is an expression of friendship between

San Diego and its Japanese sister city Yokohama that binds the two cultures to create a unique

experience for visitors from all over the world; over 240,000 people from across the United States

and the world visit the garden annually.
